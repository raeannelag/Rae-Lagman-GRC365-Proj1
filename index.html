Are You A Fairy? SZA by Chappell Roan

SZA by Chappell Roan | MUSIC

SZA has a lot on her mind—heartache, power, alienation, desire, revenge, god, water, sex, death, legacy. Across two flawless albums, Ctrl and SOS, the New Jersey native has built one of pop’s most devoted fan bases, casting spells with uncensored songs that cut as deep as the ocean that inspires so much of her work. Before hitting the road with Kendrick Lamar on the Grand National Tour, she got on the line with Chappell Roan, a kindred spirit in search of the method behind her magic.

Every rising star eventually has to face the media. For Tyriq Withers, that moment is now. So now, the people want answers: Where did he come from and how did he get here? His Him costar, the rapper Tierra Whack, came with the questions.

Who Doesn't Have a Crush On Me?

Rico Nasty by Eric Andre | MUSIC | CULTURE

Rico Nasty holds nothing back—a trait that’s earned her a rep as a rage rap pioneer. Now, Rico’s switching it up with Lethal, a grown and refined record that drops while she films her acting debut opposite Elle Fanning and Nicole Kidman in Apple TV’s Margo’s Got Money Troubles. Frankie Grande Tells His Sister Ariana How He Found His Shine Again

Frankie Grande by Ariana Grande | CULTURE | MUSIC

Frankie Grande has been through a lot: online rumor mills, a stint in the Big Brother house, and struggles with addiction. Now, at 42 years old, the former theater kid is taking those experiences and pouring them into Hotel Rock Bottom, a pop debut that provides a painful-yet-playful look at his past and has his sister Ariana bursting into tears–and song.
